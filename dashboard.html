📋 Getting Started Workflow

1
Calibrate Your Device
Use the Calibrator to personalize flex sensor ranges and IMU orientation. This ensures accurate readings for your hand.
2
Record Training Data
Use Gesture Recorder to capture labeled samples of each sign. Aim for 50-100 samples per sign for best results.
3
Train Your Model
Upload your dataset to Google Colab and run the training notebook. Export the trained model as TensorFlow.js format.
4
Start Recognition
Load your calibration and trained model into Live Sign Reader. Start recognizing signs in real-time!
